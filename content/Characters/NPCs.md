# NPCs — Frozen Sick

---

## Major NPCs

### Luney IcePeak

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **True Name** | Luney IcePeak |
| **House** | Casa IcePeak |
| **Race** | Dwarf |
| **Class** | Paladin / strategist |
| **Role** | Owner and operator of The Last Drop |

**Description:** Calm, authoritative, and prepared for the worst before anyone else realizes the worst has arrived. Luney runs the tavern as a sanctuary, a front, and a control point all at once.

**Key Facts:**
- Canonical name: **Luney IcePeak**
- Male dwarf
- Paladin
- Built hidden contingencies such as **Protocol Three**
- Keeps poison, coded diagrams, and intelligence close at hand
- Feared even by creatures like Victus

**Current Status:** Dying in a magical coma caused by Ghost Essence.

---

### Dracus ("El Manco")

One-handed warlock and old companion of Tidus. Was doing business with Luney and got tangled in Malacor's schemes. Dangerous, pragmatic, and still relevant after the tavern incident.

---

### Farquad Drasky

Military antagonist tied to Casa Draksky and larger operations beyond the tavern. Holds one of the party's secrets and remains an active threat.

---

### Malacor

Major off-screen antagonist. Commands operatives, manipulates events through proxies, and remains one of the campaign's central hostile forces.

---

### The Bounty Hunter

The pressure source at the tavern in Chapter 1. Covered in insignias, carrying legal authority and intimidation in equal measure. Survived the incident.

---

### Red / Reto

Covert operative associated with the tavern chaos and Dragonforce-style arson methods. Status remains uncertain.

---

### The Bald Enemy

Dragonforce-aligned enforcer tied to Malacor. Released Ghost Essence in the tavern and was killed by the party.

---

### Victus

Fallen paladin demon who feared Luney IcePeak because Luney had mutilated him in the past. Killed by Nixira in Chapter 3.

---

### Donner IcePeak

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Race** | Dwarf |
| **Role** | Renowned warrior, Luney's older brother |

Powerful dwarven combatant from House IcePeak. Formed a bond with Tidus during the Brasboredon escape and confirmed Luney's family connection.

**Current Status:** Alive and rallying survivors.
