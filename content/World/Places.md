## World Geography

### DragonLand (Continent / Island)

**Type:** Major landmass — the Dragonborn kingdom
**Capital:** DragonBorn (island off the eastern coast)

DragonLand is a large island continent and the primary setting of the campaign. It is the homeland of the Dragonborn and the seat of their political power. The landscape is varied: dense forests (green regions), arid plains and deserts (tan interior), mountains, and coastal settlements. A network of roads and trade routes (dashed red lines on the map) connects the major towns.

After Prince Robin (Ala Rota)'s assassination, his half-brother **Dranlek "El Oscuro"** (a.k.a. La Iguana) seized the throne and turned DragonLand into a dictatorial regime. **Dragonforce** is the kingdom's military / special forces.

**House Domains:** The Five Great Houses of DragonLand each rule a province aligned with their element:

| House | Element | Domain Province |
|-------|---------|----------------|
| **Casa Gremmory** | Fire | Noctyra |
| **Casa IcePeak** | Ice | Valdrenor |
| **DragonHeart** | Arcana | Kharvok |
| **Casa DeepWater** | Sea/Rivers | Draknor (possibly Drakmor on map) |
| **Casa Draksky** | Air/Wind | *(Union of minor lords — no single domain)* |

The **Dragon Scar** is unclaimed no-man's-land between the house domains. This is where The Last Drop tavern is located.

### DragonBorn (Island)

**Type:** Small island off the eastern coast of DragonLand
**Current Status:** The party is NOT here — they are in Brasboredon

A separate island to the east of the DragonLand mainland, connected to the Brastabro coastal area. This is the seat of Dragonborn power — the fortress-capital with castles, military barracks, a military river district, generals' mansions, an execution plaza, and military supply ships. The population speaks primarily Draconic.

**Known Infrastructure:**
- Castle with towers (Tidus's flashback: training Dragonforce companions from a castle tower)
- Military barracks ("cuartel")
- Military river district with supply ships
- Generals' mansions behind protective walls
- Execution plaza ("cadalso")
- Commercial district
- Bridge connecting districts
- Security towers with zipline-like transport systems

**City Districts (revealed in Chapter 5):**

| District | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Robinson Bridge** | Main entry point from the outer island. Where Tidus fought Borax. Green eldritch fire wall blocked passage during the revolution. |
| **Cadalso** | Execution plaza / gallows. Where public executions take place. |
| **The Mansion** | Major target for the rebel forces. Bixira was fighting wyverns here. Fireballs erupted from this location. Behind the main defense walls. |
| **Commerce (Comercio)** | Commercial district. Civilian area with shops and traders. |
| **Cuartel** | Military barracks. Dragonforce command center. Reinforcements pour from here. The barracks door was melted shut by Nixira's ballista fire — temporarily blocking reinforcements. |
| **Rivera** | Military river supply zone. Where the army receives munitions via river barges. Contains a dock with military ships. Tidus was trapped here. Has a **sacred dragon lake** enchanted with Aguas Santas (Holy Waters) that cause amnesia in non-draconic creatures. |
| **Plaza** | Central city square. |
| **Tower Network** | Watchtowers with fire ballistae, gate mechanisms, and zipline transport systems connecting towers of control. Defense walls can be activated from tower controls. |

**Defenses:**
- **Gelatina barriers** — living slime walls that emerge from the ground, sealing off entire city blocks. Transparent but impassable. Used to trap enemies and civilians alike.
- **Fire ballistae** — tower-mounted weapons loaded with barrel ammunition. Devastating area damage.
- **Gate mechanisms** — iron portcullises controlled by mechanical systems in the towers. Can be held open manually (as the orc hero did).
- **Defense walls** — activated from towers of control, block passage between districts. Cannot be opened from outside.

## Key Locations

### La ultima Gota (The Last Drop)

**Type:** Tavern / covert operations base
**Location:** Dragon Scar (no-man's-land) — geographic center of DragonLand (red tavern pin on map)
**Owner:** Luney IcePeak (Casa IcePeak)
**Chapter(s):** 1

A tavern at the heart of DragonLand that serves as a front for covert operations. Positioned centrally on the continent, making it a crossroads for travelers, merchants, and fugitives from all directions. Features hidden weapons (Protocol Three crossbows), coded service diagrams, centipede essence poison, and a famous enchanted cocktail. The party's home base and employer. Currently closed — a sign reads: *"Please don't bother. Die somewhere else."*

**Notable Features:**
- Stage for live music performances
- Kitchen (Nixira's domain)
- Hidden crossbow mechanism behind the bar (Protocol Three)
- Multiple tables with regular clientele of merchants, mercenaries, and fugitives

---

### The Mountain Pass Caves

**Type:** Natural cavern network, fortified by forest elves
**Location:** Southern mountain passage (between the tavern and the southern coast)
**Chapter(s):** 2

A vast cave system expanded and fortified by the forest elves. Water seeps from a cascading waterfall overhead. Torches line damp corridors. Infested with serpents. Contains:

- **Blood sacrifice lock** — a stone door mechanism requiring a rattlesnake's vibration to open
- **Dragon nursery** — a constructed facility for training wyrmlings, where Chimuelo was kept
- **Arcane library** — a hidden chamber full of books; a disembodied voice said "Read me, for there lies your power" and absorbed Zacarías's raven
- **Crystalline water pool** with a golden key guarded by a serpent
- **Trap gauntlet** — pressure plates, fire cannons, arrow storms, rising spikes, swinging blades
- **Iron portcullis gate** — trapped with a golden key that disintegrated Lock

**Current Status:** Farquad controls the exit. The arcane library remains unexplored.

---

### The Prison Tortuga (Giant Fire Turtle)

**Type:** Mobile prison / living dungeon
**Route:** From the mountain caves eastward toward DragonBorn island
**Chapter(s):** 3

A massive fire-elemental turtle trained to carry prisoners. The prison is built into the grooves between its colossal scales, roofed with immovable marble vaulting. Corridors feature acid glands, dart traps, pressure-plate crushing pillars, and spike-like scales. Was running at full speed toward DragonBorn.

**Notable Features:**
- Individual cells with rusted bars and padlocks
- Mimic-infested treasure rooms
- Trap corridors with darts and alarm wires
- Staging room where confiscated equipment is kept (not locked away)
- Main exit gate guarded by Branig the Double Edge

---

### The Demon's Lair (Volcanic Chamber)

**Type:** Volcanic dungeon
**Location:** Underground, along the Prison Tortuga's route toward DragonBorn
**Chapter(s):** 3

A volcanic chamber built around a lake of condemned souls. A raised platform above the magma-glow served as Victus's domain. The doors are shaped like his wings — they unlocked when he died.

**Notable Features:**
- Lake of condemned spirits (awakened by Nixira's Thaumaturgy)
- Raised combat platform
- Cauldron of corrosive volcanic poison
- Wing-shaped exit doors (unsealed after Victus's death)

### Las Tierras Lándicas (The Lándicas)

**Type:** Mystical realm / elven homeland
**Location:** Beyond DragonLand (off-map)
**Chapter(s):** 2 (described by Lock and Hook)

A mystical elven realm where no one ages and existence drifts like an endless dream. Lock and Hook came from here only 1–2 years ago. The elves left because of the Grey Dream prophecy.
