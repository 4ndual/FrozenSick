## World

**DragonLand** is not part of the continental mainland. It is a large island realm set apart across open water, with its own internal provinces, roads, forests, dry interior stretches, and mountain barriers. The campaign map shows it as a self-contained island domain rather than a continental landmass. La ultima Gota stands near the central crossroads of the island, where traffic from multiple provinces converges before continuing toward the coasts.

**DragonBorn** is the fortress-capital of DragonLand, located on a smaller island off DragonLand's eastern coast. It houses the throne, barracks, castles, execution grounds, and the political core of Dragonborn rule. When the campaign refers to carrying intelligence "to DragonBorn," it means the capital city-island, not DragonLand as a whole.

**Brasboredon** is a fortified northern settlement within DragonLand, currently defined by revolt, siege pressure, and rapidly shifting loyalties.

**The Throne of DragonLand** is contested between two half-brothers:

- **Ala Rota** (Prince Robin), the legitimate heir who was assassinated.
- **Dranlek "El Oscuro"** (La Iguana), the bastard half-brother who seized the throne and now rules as king.

After Prince Robin's death, Dranlek consolidated power from DragonBorn and turned Dragonforce into the coercive arm of his regime.

### The Five Great Houses of DragonLand

DragonLand is shaped by five separate noble houses. They are not one organization, but five distinct political powers tied to different provinces and elements:

| House | Element | Domain | Standing |
|-------|---------|--------|----------|
| **Casa Gremmory** | Fire | Noctyra | Merchant and pressure power |
| **Casa IcePeak** | Ice | Valdrenor | Noble house tied most directly to the party |
| **DragonHeart** | Arcana | Kharvok | Arcane nobility near the political core |
| **Casa DeepWater** | Sea / Rivers | Draknor | River and maritime influence |
| **Casa Draksky** | Air / Wind | Confederated territories | Union of minor lords and regional rulers |

Between those domains lies the **Dragon Scar**, the politically unstable interior belt where La ultima Gota functions as a refuge and neutral crossing point.

### The WinterSpeak Dynasty (Casa IcePeak)

The IcePeak branch is led by **Marco Claus** and **Lady Claus**. Within that dynasty, **Luney IcePeak** is the clearest canonical link to the campaign: a dwarven paladin, fixer, and operator whose reach extends well beyond the tavern.

## Prophecies

### The Grey Dream (El Sueno Gris)

**Source:** Lock and Hook (twin elves from Las Tierras Landicas)  
**First Mentioned:** Chapter 2

> *"After the death of the hybrid prince and the rise of the lying tongue, the true dragon shall come to the throne. And if the true dragon claims power, it will seek the destruction of the Tree of Life. And if the Tree of Life falls, the sleeping dragon awakens."*

The twins treat it like an old story told to children, but the campaign keeps pushing events closer to its terms.

**Possible connections:**
- "The death of the hybrid prince" -> Prince Robin's assassination
- "The rise of the lying tongue" -> Dranlek's seizure of power, or the spread of El Velo de las Siete Canciones through the courts
- "The true dragon" -> Unknown
- "The Tree of Life" -> Unknown
- "The sleeping dragon" -> Unknown catastrophic force

## Historical Events

### The Assassination of Prince Robin / Ala Rota

**When:** Before the campaign  
**Significance:** Broke the political balance of DragonLand

Prince Robin was the legitimate heir to DragonLand. His death allowed Dranlek to seize the throne at DragonBorn and convert the kingdom into a dictatorship.

**Secret:** Tidus killed Prince Roto, heir to the Dragonborn throne.

### The Fall of the Southern Elven Tribe

**When:** Around 90 years before the campaign  
**Significance:** Foundational trauma in Tidus's history

Tidus's people were destroyed through betrayal rather than open military defeat. Survivors were displaced northward, and the event still shapes his distrust and survival instincts.

### The Corruption of El Velo de las Siete Canciones

**When:** More than 5 years before the campaign  
**Significance:** Turned a healing order into a covert political weapon

Someone close to Kaelan Thorne "La Cuerda Rota" twisted El Velo de las Siete Canciones away from palliative care and into a network of infiltration and influence. Nixira fled, Kaelan Thorne "La Cuerda Rota" framed her, and the order has hunted her since.

## Key Artifacts and Items

### Corona Dorada

**Holder:** Malacor / Farquad  
**Type:** Mind-control artifact

A crown-like command artifact that enforces obedience and destabilizes every power structure it touches.

### Ghost Essence

**Type:** Cursed substance  
**Effect:** Pulls the strongest spiritual presence into a deathly sleep

Used against Luney IcePeak at the tavern. The cure requires ground emerald.

### The Arcane Library

**Location:** Hidden chamber in the mountain pass caves  
**Type:** Unknown magical repository

A room of arcane texts tied to a disembodied voice that consumed Zacarias's raven familiar.

Recovered from a trapped room in the caves.

### Blood-Web Shield

## Magical Concepts

### Ghost Essence Curse

A living shadow-ink curse that drags its victim into a magical coma.

### The Don

The musical gift used by El Velo de las Siete Canciones. It can ease pain or manipulate minds, depending on who wields it and why.

### Malfas's Patronage

Zacarias's pact grants power, protection, and a steady stream of costly consequences.

### Condemned Spirits

Spirits trapped beneath the volcanic lake under the demon's lair. Their full role is still unknown.
