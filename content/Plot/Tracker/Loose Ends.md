# Loose Ends

## Tidus - Why Did He Kill Prince Roto?

- **What:** Tidus's deepest secret is that he assassinated Prince Roto, heir to the DragonBorn throne. But he is **Chaotic Good**: loyal, protective of innocents, and merciful in combat.
- **Questions:** Was it an order from within Dragonforce? Was Prince Roto corrupt, dangerous, or about to cause something worse? Was Tidus manipulated? Is this connected to the Grey Dream prophecy?
- **Why it matters:** This act helped trigger Dranlek "El Oscuro" (La Iguana)'s rise and could reshape the meaning of the prophecy itself.

## Zacarias - Why Did He Fall from Paladin to Warlock?

- **What:** Zacarias was once a paladin with holy abilities and divine protection. He lost those gifts and entered a pact with Malfas, Prince of Ravens.
- **Questions:** Was it connected to Malacor? To the Golden Crown? Did he break an oath, or was he forced out of grace?
- **Why it matters:** The truth of the fall defines whether his arc is about redemption, revenge, or acceptance.

## Who Knows Tidus Killed Prince Roto?

- **What:** If this secret reaches the wrong ears in DragonLand, Tidus becomes one of the most wanted people in the kingdom.
- **Questions:** Did Borax tell anyone before he died? Does Farquad know? Does Dragonforce keep records that can still expose Tidus?
- **Risk:** Extreme.

## The Beautiful Elf Woman - Tidus's Past

- **What:** Tidus once traveled the caves with a beautiful elf woman he taught the "survival dance."
- **Questions:** Who is she? Is she alive? Is she tied to Dragonforce, the Veil, or Tidus's missing history?

## Who is Drogo?

- **What:** Dracus mentioned Drogo, supposedly away on a mission to mount a red dragon.
- **Status:** Off-screen and potentially important.

## Nixira's Twin Sister

- **What:** Nixira's backstory suggests she may have a twin sister.
- **Questions:** Does the twin exist? Is she alive? Is she tied to El Velo de las Siete Canciones?

## The Vanished Compass

- **What:** Zacarias's raven chose a moonstone over a trapped compass in the caves. The compass vanished by hidden mechanism.
- **Questions:** Why was the compass protected? Where did it go? What does it point to?

## Where Are Senor Nadie and Fuercitas?

- **What:** They escaped the demon's lair with the party, then vanished from later beats.
- **Questions:** Were they separated during the missing gap? Are they still alive? Are they somewhere in Brasboredon or beyond?

## What Did the Centinelas de la Era Blanca Sentinel Report?

- **What:** A sentinel fled toward DragonBorn carrying intelligence.
- **Questions:** Did that report include Tidus's identity? Has Dragonforce already acted on it?

## Luney's Endgame

- **What:** Luney ran La ultima Gota as more than a tavern: poison work, hidden weapons, rare herb trade, and intelligence all flowed through it.
- **Questions:** Who did he truly serve? Was the Ghost Essence attack meant specifically to neutralize him?

## Rainbow - Nixira's Rival and Secret-Keeper

- **What:** Rainbow knows Nixira's secret and may still be alive.
- **Status:** Uncertain.

## Red - Tidus's Secret-Keeper

- **What:** Red held Tidus's secret and was tied to Dragonforce-style arson work.
- **Status:** Uncertain.

## Red's Gunpowder

- **What:** Gunpowder trails were laid across the tavern floor and never fully neutralized.
- **Status:** The tavern is closed, but the threat remains.

## Kahel Varos - Who Sent Him?

- **What:** Kahel Varos, "El Heraldo," died in Chapter 1. The active question is no longer whether he drank poison, but who deployed him to La ultima Gota in the first place.
- **Status:** Active lead.
- **Questions:** Who sent him, and what broader operation was already in motion before his death?

## Dracus's Fate After the Ambush

- **What:** Dracus was trying to free Tidus during Farquad's ambush at the cave exit.
- **Status:** Unknown.

## The Fat Merchant

## Hook - Alone and Traumatized
