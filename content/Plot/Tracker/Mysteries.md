# Mysteries

## The Fallen Prince

- **What:** Victus's god tasked him with destroying the Fallen Prince, and he failed.
- **Questions:** Who is the Fallen Prince now? Was Victus hunting the wrong target, or did someone survive?

## The Mysterious Woman

- **What:** Pale skin, crimson eyes, blood-red dress, frostlike sparkles.
- **Questions:** Ally, enemy, vampire, dhampir, or something stranger?

## The Voice in the Arcane Library

- **What:** "Read me, for there lies your power."
- **Questions:** What entity consumed Zacarias's raven? What power does it offer, and what will it cost?

## Luney's True Nature

- **What:** Luney once cut the wings off a demon and left Victus terrified of him.
- **Questions:** How powerful is Luney really? What else has he done? What is he hiding behind the tavern facade?

## Tidus's Deity Response

- **What:** When Tidus called upon Bhaal in Prison Tortuga, he heard: "Do you know how to awaken the darkness?"
- **Questions:** What darkness is Bhaal referring to? Is it tied to the Grey Dream prophecy?

## The Grey Dream's Progress

- **What:** The prophecy warns of a true dragon seeking the throne, destroying the Tree of Life, and awakening the sleeping dragon.
- **Questions:** Which conditions are already in motion? Who is the true dragon? Is Dranlek "El Oscuro" the lying tongue?

## The Revolution - Who Started It?

- **What:** Brasboredon erupted into organized rebellion.
- **Questions:** Was Bixira only one node in a larger network? Who sits above her in the chain of command?

## Bixira's Portal (Plano Oscuro)

- **What:** The clash between Bixira's soul-light and the General's meteorite opened an interdimensional portal.
- **Status:** Unstable and unexplained.
- **Questions:** Was it only an escape breach, or the beginning of a larger planar threat?

## The Bomba de Oscuridad

- **What:** Tidus carries a device that sends unprotected living targets into the Dark Dimension.
- **Status:** Still in his possession.
- **Questions:** Is it a weapon, a prison sentence, or a doorway to something worse?

## Robinson - Presumed Dead

- **What:** Robinson vanished in the blast at the gatehouse.
- **Questions:** Is he truly dead?

## Kaelan Thorne "La Cuerda Rota" - Alive and Commanding El Velo de las Siete Canciones

- **What:** Believed dead for two years, now seen alive through Draxa's portal directing the Veil from a silver throne.
- **Status:** Active threat.
- **Questions:** Why was his death faked? What is his endgame? When will he move directly against Nixira?

## Draxa - Wounded Veil Operative

- **What:** Draxa survived the gate battle and escaped through the portal after taking massive damage.
- **Status:** Alive and likely to return.
- **Questions:** What is her full power set? How far does her authority inside the Veil reach?

## Zacarias - Magical Dystrophy

- **What:** His magic started failing mid-battle, and his body began to give out.
- **Status:** Active debuff.
- **Questions:** Is the decay tied to Malfas, resurrection, or the false prophet mission?

## Bixira's Last Warning

- **What:** "No dejes que el Senor de los Centinelas de la Era Blanca tome el trono."
- **Status:** Unresolved.
- **Questions:** Who is that lord? Is he tied to the Kahel Varos thread, DragonBorn politics, or something even larger?
