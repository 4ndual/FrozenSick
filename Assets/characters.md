### Zacarías Belmonte

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Race** | Gnome (magically appears ~2m tall — his true nature shocked the party when revealed) |
| **Class** | Warlock (Pact of the Chain) / Former Paladin |
| **Patron** | Malfas (Melfas), the Prince of Ravens |
| **Tavern Role** | Doorman / Bouncer at La Última Gota |

**Personality:** Resourceful and scheming ("I always have a plan"). Ultra-chaotic by his own admission. Reckless to the point his companions call him suicidal. Manipulative — freely uses deception and trickery. Greedy — instinctively gravitates toward loot. Despite his self-serving nature, ultimately loyal to the group.

**Backstory:** Once a paladin who lost his holy abilities and divine protection. Made a pact with Malfas, Prince of Ravens, gaining a raven familiar and warlock powers. Was questioned and underestimated because of being a gnome, which drove his ambition. Was once close friends with Malacor/Malaquías — they shared a faith or order — but something went wrong between them. Denies giving Malacor "the golden crown." The raven that occasionally visited the tavern was always his familiar in disguise. Speaks "picón" (a shared secret language) with Tidus.

**Abilities:**
- Raven familiar (can see through its eyes, scout remotely)
- Pocket Dimension (teleport familiar around the battlefield)
- Minor Illusion, Phantom Sound
- Illusion magic (conjured rabbit and cow illusions to trigger traps)
- Blood-web shield (17 charges, granted by Malfas at the cost of ritual casting)
- Dagger combat with poison application
- Stealth and sneak attacks
- Speak with Animals (communicated with rats and serpents in the caves)
- Serpentine language ("Pársel")
- Animal Handling

**Key Story Arc:**
1. **Tavern:** Received a secret warning note. Attempted to haul water to neutralize gunpowder but slipped and fell. Activated Protocol Three (hidden crossbows).
2. **Combat Aftermath:** Revealed his gnome identity and connection to Malfas. Summoned a giant desert boa to dispose of the bald enemy's corpse. Stole coins using his raven-turned-mouse.
3. **Cave Exploration:** Used raven to discover the door mechanism, a moonstone (10 platinum), and the dragon nursery. Found cave maps and climbing equipment. Used Speak with Animals to map serpent positions through rats. Spoke to serpents in Pársel. Lost his raven familiar in a mysterious arcane library where a voice said "Read me, for there lies your power." Tricked the twin elf Hook into believing his brother Lock was teleported (not killed).
4. **Turtle Prison:** Broke out of his cell using bed-frame rods. Summoned raven to scout (it was dissolved by acid). Called upon Malfas — received the blood-web shield. Allied with the Nameless Monk. Navigated trap corridors using illusions and strategy. Encountered a mysterious pale woman with crimson eyes at the session's cliffhanger.
5. **Demon Lair:** Poisoned his daggers with corrosive venom from the demon's cauldron. Delivered a devastating critical backstab on the demon. Got grabbed, stabbed with his own burning dagger, and knocked to **0 HP**. Making death saving throws as the session ends.

**Current Status:** Unconscious (0 HP), reached **Level 3**. Bald (scalp ripped off by the demon). Lost raven familiar (dissolved by acid). Carrying 16 blood-shield charges and 100 copper pieces.

### Tidus

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Race** | Elf or Half-Elf (treated as "brother of race" by forest elf guards) |
| **Class** | Ranger / Rogue (multiclass traits) |
| **Tavern Role** | Waiter at La Última Gota |

**Personality:** Pragmatic, impatient, and blunt ("I don't care about your problems, we need to move"). A natural leader who makes decisive calls under pressure. Intensely loyal despite friction with companions. Reflexively violent when startled — puts a knife to throats before thinking. Has a romantic streak (fond memories of a beautiful elf from his past).

**Backstory:** Former member of **Dragonforce**, a criminal/military organization. Recognizes their signature assassination-by-arson technique from personal experience. Old companion of Drac — they ran many missions together. Speaks "picón" with Zacarías, suggesting a shared cultural background. Has dragon-riding training from his past. Once traveled the caves with a beautiful elf woman he was fond of and taught her the "survival dance." Has worked at the tavern for approximately 2 years. Also described with fire-related traits (sparks from his nose when stressed), suggesting possible draconic heritage.

**Abilities:**
- High Persuasion (+5), Survival expertise
- Stealth and sneak attacks
- Dagger combat with assassination technique (ongoing bleed damage)
- "Survival dance" (prevents serpent attacks in caves)
- Thaumaturgy (possibly item-based or multiclass)
- Dragon taming (physical approach — silence, touch, feeding)

**Key Story Arc:**
1. **Tavern:** Delivered poisoned "special orders" (prepared by Nixira) to targets including Caelvaros. Recognized gunpowder trails from Dragonforce's arson method. Tackled and knocked out Dracus when he drew a dagger — but was spotted by Farquad, triggering a chase by Dracus's 5 allies.
2. **Combat Aftermath:** Got the killing blow on the bald enemy (broke his neck while the enemy bled out). Looted a scimitar, light crossbow, gold, and silver. Nearly killed by crossbow shot before using a super healing potion.
3. **Cave Exploration:** Remembered the cave from a past journey. **Tamed a baby rock dragon (wyrmling) named Line** by approaching silently, touching its nose, and feeding it. Rode Line through the cave. Directed rescue of 23 trapped people. **Captured** at the cave exit by Farquad's ambush.
4. **Turtle Prison:** Paralyzed during capture but remained aware through a slipped blindfold. Called upon his deity for wisdom, heard "Do you know how to awaken the darkness?" Broke his shackle chain.
5. **Demon Lair:** Attempted to negotiate with the demon Raku/Victus, asking "Do you know Rubén?" Trapped by the demon's Air Prison. Used crossbow in combat.

**Current Status:** Alive, bruised but standing. Reached **Level 3**. Owns a scimitar and light crossbow from earlier loot.

### Nixira Silverson / Kenitzira / Mai Melody / Melo

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Race** | Tiefling ("my lineage doesn't control me") |
| **Class** | Bard (Cornetist / Musician) with combat proficiency (crossbow, daggers) |
| **Tavern Role** | Cook and House band leader at La Última Gota |

**Personality:** Emotional but tough. Fiery — literally has fire in her eyes when provoked, and small horns emerge when angered. Dramatic and proud with strong stage presence. Deeply loyal to friends. Competitive (bitter rivalry with Rainbow). Hot-headed and honor-obsessed — challenges others to dragon chess when offended. Stubborn but forgiving, genuinely moved by apologies. Self-deprecating about her cooking versus her music. Creative in combat — uses multiple voices and psychological warfare through music. Carries deep guilt and fear about her past. Brave enough to reveal her secret history to the group.

**Backstory:** A famous cornetist not heard publicly in over 5 years. "Mai Melody" is her stage name; her real name is **Kenitzira**. Former member of the **Veil of the Seven Songs** — a secret society of musical witches that originally used mental manipulation through music for palliative care (easing suffering of the magically ill). The organization was corrupted by someone close to **Keylan**, who weaponized their powers to infiltrate the politics of every kingdom, placing agents on every throne. Nixira fled when she discovered the corruption. **Keylan betrayed and framed her**, and the Veil is hunting her. She has hidden at the tavern for ~2 years, working in the kitchen where no one sees her. Her identity was exposed during a performance. Possibly has a twin sister.

**Abilities:**
- Thaumaturgy (tiefling racial — voice amplification, multiple echoing voices, floating flames)
- Minor Illusion
- Firebolt cantrip
- Bardic Inspiration
- Vicious Mockery / vocal attacks ("vociferación")
- "Bloom Boys" (magical voice amplification — 3x volume for 1 minute)
- Persuasion +5, Deception +5
- Mental manipulation through music (the "don" from the Veil — extent of power unclear)
- Crossbow combat, dagger combat
- Instruments: harp, cornet, mini percussion
- Dragon chess challenges (honor-based conflict resolution)

**Key Story Arc:**
1. **Tavern:** Prepared poisoned "special orders" using centipede essence in the kitchen. Announced on stage, triggering Rainbow's fury (his companions had to physically restrain him). Locked eyes with Rainbow defiantly. Orchestrated a "salon dance" using Bloom Boys to clear the room, giving Lunei Milei Maili's crossbows clean firing lines. Shifted music tempo to de-escalate violence.
2. **Combat Aftermath:** Used Thaumaturgy to capture enemy attention. Organized musical march to surround enemies. Cast Firebolt and threw daggers. Revealed her real identity as Kenitzira. Felt she could no longer stay at the tavern. Has a list of **24 people** from Lunei Milei Maili's pocket.
3. **Cave Exploration:** Used Thaumaturgy to create floating flame (distracted serpent). Tried Minor Illusion to fake serpent voice — backfired because serpents are solitary; got bitten. Used Persuasion to make Zacarías feel genuine guilt ("the Grinch moment"). Revealed her full backstory to the party. **Serpent venom: ~22 hours until anaphylactic reaction.** Shot a serpent with crossbow in crystalline water to retrieve a golden key. Wore a decapitated serpent as a belt. Helped pull a lever to raise an iron gate (rolled 19). Status unclear in Farquad's ambush.
4. **Turtle Prison:** Struggled to break out of her cell. Fought **two mimics** — incinerated one with Firebolt, escaped the other by tickling its tongue-choking attack. Retrieved keys from mimics. Freed monk pupils. Witnessed Rodrigo's death (darts through the neck) and vowed to write a song for him. Confronted Branig the Double Edge (undead guard) at the main gate.
5. **Demon Lair:** Played calming harp to give Tidus advantage on charisma checks. Switched to war drums for combat. Used Thaumaturgy with multiple echoing voices to psychologically torment the demon, awakening **condemned spirits** who spoke to him — including the voice of his god. Kicked the demon for 1 damage. **Delivered the killing blow** with a crossbow bolt through the demon's skull, dedicating it to Zacarías.

**Current Status:** Alive but injured (took a hit to the face, lost singing voice temporarily). Reached **Level 3**. Serpent venom timer may still be running. Identity exposed — the Veil of the Seven Songs could be tracking her.

|--------|------|
| **Species** | Giant red/fire turtle — trained prison transport |
| **Description** | Enormous red-scaled turtle with nearly indestructible scales. Natural traps (acid secretions, spike-like scales, pressure-plate bleeding glands). The prison is built into the gaps between its scales. |

**Key Detail:** Running at full speed toward "DragonLand." If the guards are killed, it will stop to "think" — buying time.

**Current Status:** Active, running at full speed.

Shape-shifting predatory creatures disguised as treasure chests. Used as prison security. At least 2–3 encountered and destroyed. One could acquire material properties (turned shiny, absorbed a key). Dropped keys and a gem (5 silver).
