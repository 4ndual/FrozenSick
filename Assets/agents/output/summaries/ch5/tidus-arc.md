## Chapter 5 — The Escape from Brasboredon

Tidus led a desperate dash across a killing field in the Rivera district, accompanied by Captain Donner IcePeak and a squad of dwarven soldiers (Messi, Cory, and Achawn). Under the constant threat of the legendary "Sniper," Tidus coordinated the use of smoke bombs and shield formations to advance through the streets. During the chaotic retreat, he witnessed a harrowing sight: the bodies of three dwarf children and their father, victims of the city's collapse. The tension peaked when a fire arrow exploded against Cory’s shield, leaving the dwarf exposed; Tidus watched in horror as a follow-up shot struck Cory between the eyes, killing him instantly.

Despite the loss, Tidus pushed the survivors forward, leap-frogging through smoke clouds and rubble. He accidentally activated his communication stone, later using it to alert Zacarías and Nixira of the sniper’s lethal range and the "massacre" unfolding in the streets. From his position, he witnessed the cataclysmic battle at the Mansion, where Bixira’s soul seemingly tore open an interdimensional portal to the dark plane to intercept a volcanic meteor. At the session's end, Tidus remained with the remaining squad, reporting his location and preparing for the final push out of the burning city.
