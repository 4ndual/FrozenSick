## Chapter 5 — The Escape from Brasboredon

Nixira regained consciousness outside the city walls following the failed explosion, initially mourning Robinson’s apparent death amidst the rubble. After locating Zacarías through a stone grate, she used *Vociferación* to shout *¡Zacarías! ¿Cómo estás?* and reassured him of his place in the party: *No te preocupes, Zacarías, te aceptaremos tal y como eres, un Warlock.* Coordination became key as she used *Mage Hand* (Mano de Mago) to help Zacarías operate a massive pulley mechanism, successfully hoisting a heavy iron gate that blocked their path. She later guided him through a narrow crawlspace beneath a diagonal reja using her magical communication stone, warning him of a nearby Troll and a battalion of dwarves engaged in a desperate "Group Wave" attack.

The weight of the battle took an emotional toll when Nixira overheard the sounds of slaughter and the deaths of companions through Tidus’s activated walkie-talkie. Overcome by grief and the sighting of the powerful Heraldo de las Siete Canciones at the palace, she sought comfort by singing *Duality* into her stone to honor the fallen, an act that unexpectedly inspired the nearby enanos. Her resolve was tested further in a brutal duel against a Dragon Trainer who seared her face with acidic spores. Nixira laughed through the pain, countered with a regeneration potion, and mocked her foe in sign language: *Deberíamos besarnos para romper la tensión.*

In the heat of combat, she pinned the trainer’s weaponized chain to the ground with *Mage Hand* and cast *Dissonant Whispers* to deafen him with a high-pitched note. She executed a perfect leg sweep to bring the disoriented enemy down and attempted to drive a dagger into his neck, though the trainer managed to kick her back and partially free his weapon. As the session ended, Nixira watched Bixira’s soul-light clash with a falling volcanic meteor that opened an interdimensional portal over Brasboredon. Exhausted and solitary, she began navigating the vine-covered walls of the Mansion, determined to regroup and survive the cataclysm.
