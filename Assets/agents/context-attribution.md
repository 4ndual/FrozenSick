## Voice Profiles (Summary)

### DM (Frank/Franklin)

**Strongest signals:**
- Second-person narration: "Ves como...", "Te das cuenta...", "Alcanzas a ver..."
- Dice requests: "Lanza un dado de 20", "La prueba es 15"
- Turn prompts: "[Name] qué quieres hacer?"
- Scene descriptions: distances in pies/casillas, terrain details
- NPC voicing: shifts to first person when speaking as an NPC

**DM voicing NPCs — signals:**
- Usually preceded by context: "Donner te dice..." or "Messi se acerca y dice..."
- NPC speech is often in response to a PC's question
- The DM's NPC voice returns to narrator voice for consequences

### Nixira (Selene)

**Strongest signals:**
- Mano de Mago usage (ONLY she uses this regularly)
- Dramatic/emotional action descriptions
- References to Robinson (her combat companion)
- Vicious Mockery / freestyle rap combat
- "Me levanto... me quedo viendo..." — descriptive roleplay
- Mourning/emotional outbursts

### Zacarías (Morty/Johan)

**Strongest signals:**
- Raven familiar commands ("mi cuervo", "mi cuervito")
- Multi-step tactical plans: "Bueno, voy a hacer lo siguiente..."
- Chaotic improvisation: unconventional spell uses
- References to his patron Malfas
- "Tengo que intentarlo" — impulsive decisions
- Interacts with dwarves/enanos (protective pact)

### Tidus (Andrés/Andual)

**Strongest signals:**
- Terse, direct declarations: "Avanzo." "Lo mato."
- Sneak Attack in combat
- Lockpicking/trap disarming
- "No tengo tiempo para esto" — impatient pragmatism
- Communication stone (piedrita) — voice from another location
- Crossbow precision shots
