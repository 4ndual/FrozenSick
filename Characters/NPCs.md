### Lunei Milei Maili (Rudolph IcePeak)

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **True Name** | **Rudolph IcePeak** — Lord of Commerce (Señor del Comercio) |
| **House** | **Casa IcePeak** (WinterSpeak Dynasty, Ice element, Lords of Valdrenor) |
| **Race** | Not confirmed |
| **Class** | Invoker / Paladin / Unknown 3rd class (multi-class) |
| **Role** | Owner and operator of La Última Gota tavern |
| **Note** | Name is frequently garbled by voice-to-text as Lunay, Luna, Unai, Leonel, Leo, etc. |

**Description:** The boss. Authoritative, strategic, and calm under pressure. Runs the tavern as a front for covert operations. Has extensive contingency protocols (Protocol Three: hidden crossbows behind the bar). Wealthy enough to sacrifice 500 gold in bottles as a distraction. Known for his cocktail "Espíritus del Bosque" (Spirits of the Forest) — normal water imbued with your saddest memory's magic, designed to make customers melancholic so they drink more.

**Backstory:** Once encountered a lesser demon who stole from him — he **cut off the demon's wing** as a warning, establishing himself as formidable and ruthless. Has many shady business dealings. Maintained a list of people. Trades with the twin elves for rare herbs. Has connections to multiple organizations.

**Key Events:**
- Activated Protocol Three during the tavern incident — smashed bottles, revealed four hidden crossbows.
- Provided centipede essence poison for the special orders.
- Hit by ghost essence attack, fell into a magical coma / oneiric dream state.
- Appeared to the PCs in a dark dreamscape, walking through a black swamp.
- Told the PCs he needs a **ground emerald** within "two quarters" or he will die.
- Directed them to go to **River** or the **Elven Camp** for help.
- The demon Victus feared him — Lunei is the one who cut off Victus's wings as punishment.

**Current Status:** **Dying** — in a magical coma. Needs a ground emerald within a time limit to survive.

### Drac / Dracus ("El Manco")

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Race** | Not confirmed (possibly human) |
| **Class** | Warlock (Pact of the Blade) |
| **Description** | Thin/slight build, one-handed ("el manco") |

**Personality:** Dangerous but pragmatic. Afraid of Tidus but respects him. Playful in a menacing way. Dark sense of humor.

**Backstory:** Old companion of Tidus from their mission days together. His Pact weapon can dematerialize and rematerialize at will. Was doing business with Lunei Milei Maili and got entangled with Malacor's interference. Mentions **Drogo** — someone currently on a mission to mount a red dragon. His last mission involved killing 150 goblins to protect a village.

**Key Events:**
- **Ch.1 — Tavern:** Sat silently refusing to order, watching. Pulled a dagger during chaos. Knocked unconscious by Tidus, who recognized his intent. His 5 allies rallied against Tidus.
- **Ch.2 — Caves:** Found alone, pressed against a wall, terrified. Revealed that Malacor is using the golden crown to control people. Agreed to alliance with Tidus against Malacor. Was trying to free Tidus during Farquad's ambush.

**Relationships:** Tidus (old mission companion), Lunei (business partner), Malacor (entangled), The Fat Merchant (calls Dracus his "guardian")

**Current Status:** Alive. Was attempting rescue when the ambush struck. Fate uncertain.

### Borax — The Envious Apprentice

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Race** | Black Dragonborn |
| **Class** | Warlock (demonic/otherworldly pact) |
| **Former Role** | DragonForce apprentice / castle servant |

**Description:** Once a pathetic, thin, weak apprentice mage who couldn't produce a single spark during DragonForce training. Mocked by the entire castle. After making a demonic pact, gained the ability to cast green eldritch fire from both hands. Wore a **talisman** that incrementally filled with stolen power — trading his soul for fire. At 6/6, the pact entities would have taken full control of his body.

**Backstory:** Served Tidus breakfast in the castle on the morning Prince Roto was assassinated. Tidus showed him a rare moment of compassion — touching his head and saying *"Gracias. I recommend you leave the castle."* Despite this, Borax harbored deep envy and resentment toward Tidus, who succeeded at everything Borax couldn't.

**Key Events:**
- **Ch.4:** Blocked Robinson Bridge with a wall of green fire. Confronted Tidus. Absorbed wall flames to heal (draconic regeneration through life sacrifice). Talisman reached 5/6 when Tidus's crossbow bolt shattered it. Body melted into a charred stone mass. A **fire elemental** erupted from the remains but was destroyed when Tidus threw a dagger into the stone.

**Relationships:** Tidus (old rival — envied him), DragonForce (former apprentice)

**Current Status:** **Dead.** Body reduced to charred stone on Robinson Bridge.

### Donner IcePeak

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **True Name** | Donner IcePeak |
| **House** | **Casa IcePeak** (Ice element, Lords of Valdrenor) |
| **Race** | Dwarf |
| **Class** | Martial-magical hybrid (Eldritch Knight or similar) |
| **Role** | Renowned warrior, Lunei's older brother |

**Description:** Recognized across every continent with an education system. Uses an esgrima needle that transforms into a greatsword. Carries the **Rito** enchantment — a quartz-activated spell that turns his skin crystalline like diamond armor. One of eight IcePeak brothers. Celebrated, proud, emotionally volatile when it comes to his family.

**Key Events:**
- **Ch.5:** Accompanied Tidus through the Sniper crossing and the Gremory market. Led the escort (Messi, Achawn, Cory, Sid). Coordinated shield formations under Sniper's fire. Briefed Tidus on Sniper's capabilities: *"No son varios, solo es Sniper."*
- Received a quartz fragment from Berlin that activated his **Rito shield** — crystalline armor.
- **Briefly possessed by two deities** (Val and Moradin) after Altaflama's death curse. Hurled a sword at Nixira while under possession. Saved by Tidus's medallion.
- Revealed as **Lunei's brother** — furious that Lunei was left dying of Frigid Woe in Eiselcross. *"¿Por qué demonios dejaste a mi hermano en medio de Eiselcross?"*
- Gave Tidus an **IcePeak favor coin** — valid with any of his seven brothers.
- Delivered an emotional speech about the revolution: *"Eres un símbolo... no dejes que muera la esperanza."*
- Offered one final advantage: his Rito shield can absorb 3–4 of Sniper's shots.

**Relationships:** Lunei (brother — one of eight), Tidus (primary ally in Ch.5), House Gremory (by reputation), Berlin (employer connection)

**Current Status:** Alive. Confronted Tidus about the failed revolution — slapped him, called him a fool for only killing the leader. Wept over the radio confirmations of casualties. Gave Tidus the **IcePeak favor coin** and departed: *"No dejes que muera la esperanza. La esperanza es Lunei y no soy yo."* Later sent reinforcements toward the gate when the party called for help against Draxa. Currently rallying the surviving dwarven forces.
