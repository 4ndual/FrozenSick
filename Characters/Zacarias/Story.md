# Zacarías Belmonte — Story Arc

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Race** | Gnome (magically appears ~2m tall — his true nature shocked the party when revealed) |
| **Class** | Warlock (Pact of the Chain) / Former Paladin |
| **Patron** | Malfas (Melfas), the Prince of Ravens |
| **Tavern Role** | Doorman / Bouncer at La Última Gota |

**Personality:** Resourceful and scheming ("I always have a plan"). Ultra-chaotic by his own admission. Reckless to the point his companions call him suicidal. Manipulative — freely uses deception and trickery. Greedy — instinctively gravitates toward loot. Despite his self-serving nature, ultimately loyal to the group.

**Backstory:** Once a paladin who lost his holy abilities and divine protection. Made a pact with Malfas, Prince of Ravens, gaining a raven familiar and warlock powers. Was questioned and underestimated because of being a gnome, which drove his ambition. Was once close friends with Malacor/Malaquías — they shared a faith or order — but something went wrong between them. Denies giving Malacor "the golden crown." The raven that occasionally visited the tavern was always his familiar in disguise. Speaks "picón" (a shared secret language) with Tidus.

**Abilities:**
- Raven familiar (can see through its eyes, scout remotely)
- Pocket Dimension (teleport familiar around the battlefield)
- Minor Illusion, Phantom Sound
- Illusion magic (conjured rabbit and cow illusions to trigger traps)
- Blood-web shield (17 charges, granted by Malfas at the cost of ritual casting)
- Dagger combat with poison application
- Stealth and sneak attacks
- Speak with Animals (communicated with rats and serpents in the caves)
- Serpentine language ("Pársel")
- Animal Handling

## Chapter 1 — The Tavern

Received a secret warning note from Lunei hidden under a drink. Attempted to haul water to neutralize the gunpowder but slipped and fell spectacularly in the kitchen. Part of the Protocol Three activation (hidden crossbows).

In the combat aftermath, revealed his gnome identity and connection to Malfas. Summoned a giant desert boa to dispose of the bald enemy's corpse. Stole coins using his raven-turned-mouse. Confessed his past connection to Malacor.

## Chapter 2 — The Plateau

Used raven to discover the door mechanism, a moonstone (10 platinum), and the dragon nursery. Found cave maps and climbing equipment. Used Speak with Animals to map serpent positions through rats. Spoke to serpents in Pársel. Lost his raven familiar in a mysterious arcane library where a voice said "Read me, for there lies your power." Tricked the twin elf Hook into believing his brother Lock was teleported (not killed). Knocked unconscious by Farquad's Level 15 elven tactician during the ambush.

## Chapter 3 — The Flying Turtle

Broke out of his cell using bed-frame rods. Summoned raven to scout (it was dissolved by acid). Called upon Malfas — received the blood-web shield (17 charges) at the cost of all ritual casting. Allied with the Nameless Monk (Señor Nadie). Navigated trap corridors using illusions and strategy. Encountered a mysterious pale woman with crimson eyes at a cliffhanger moment.

In the demon's lair, poisoned his daggers with corrosive venom from the demon's cauldron. Delivered a devastating critical backstab on the demon. Got grabbed, stabbed with his own burning dagger, and knocked to **0 HP**. Scalp ripped off by the demon. Making death saving throws as the session ends.

## Chapter 4 — The Battle of Dragon Born

Entered Dragon Born city through the main gate during the revolution. Summoned raven familiar to scout from above — witnessed the massacre: twenty children of all races dead in the streets, dwarven mothers wailing over charred bones. Initially called for "guerra muerte contra los dragones" over the communication stone.

After seeing the bodies and being challenged by Tidus, delivered an **"I Have a Dream" speech** — a moment of moral clarity about racial harmony, refusing to repeat the cycles of hatred. *"Todos podemos ser diferentes. No tenemos que armar una guerra y repetir la historia."* Closed with the draconic prayer: *"Todos nacemos del fuego y morimos en el fuego."* Malfas responded with ravens. Gained **1 point of inspiration**.

Mapped the city zones: Cadalso (gallows), Mansión, Comercio (commerce), Cuartel (barracks), Plaza, Rivera (military supply). Regrouped with Nixira and Robinson at the gatehouse tower. Used **Hellish Rebuke** (three fire rays) to shatter the magical shield protecting three draconid archers who were targeting the orc gate-holder. Distracted the archers with his raven familiar — one raven died in the attack.

After the party was separated by gates and gelatina barriers, ended up **trapped alone** near the Rivera district. Encountered a **stone invoker** — a draconid elementalist with earth manipulation powers and **psychic immunity**. Tried Command ("Aléjate") — bounced harmlessly off a forehead mark. Attempted Burning Hands in melee — the draconid **ripped off Zacarías's right hand**. Lost the hand that channels warlock pact magic. Threw remaining dagger with left hand. Took multiple kicks to the face. Down to ~9 HP with a raven familiar barely alive.

Meanwhile, Tidus found a trapped chest on a rooftop. Opened it with lockpicks, found stones inside. Pushed it off a trapdoor and heard someone calling for help — the session ended with Tidus attempting to disarm the bomb mechanism.
